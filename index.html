Welcome to Squid Type! To get started, click the "host" button to host a lobby or the "join" button, along with the host's code, to join another person's lobby.
The objective of this game is to type all of the displayed words faster than your opponent. Be careful of the traffic light, though...

When the light is green, you're free to type. Go as fast as you can for the finish line!
When the light is yellow, you can keep typing, but be ready to stop before the light changes again.
When the light is red, STOP typing. If you type when the light is red, you're out!
